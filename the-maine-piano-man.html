Learn more about The Maine Piano Man

The Maine Piano Man is a Maine-based piano, organ, and keyboard performer with more than 50 years of playing experience and over 40 years of piano tuning expertise.

Performing more than 800 songs across multiple genres, he brings a versatile live set suited for all types of gigs, from standards and jazz to pop, rock, blues, classical, and original music.

Whether entertaining a crowd, providing background atmosphere, or bringing classic favorites to life, The Maine Piano Man delivers seasoned musicianship and a lifelong passion for performance.
