Learn more about Don Blauss

Don recently moved to Maine and brings many years of experience performing both solo and in bands.

His background spans classic rock and country, with a deep catalog of songs covering decades of music.

With a repertoire focused on the 60’s through early 2000’s, Don blends Americana, folk-rock, and singer/songwriter styles into his performances.
