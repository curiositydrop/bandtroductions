Learn more about Wildfeuer

We are a four piece New England rock band that has an expansive variety of music, experience in the underground and more public music scene and is constantly creating old songs featured deeply personal lyrics and soundscapes that we feel will connect with a wide audience.

We are always open to new experiences, collaborations, shows and broadening our horizons. Give us a listen and let us know what you think =]
