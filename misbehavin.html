Learn more about MISBEHAVIN

MISBEHAVIN is a five-piece female-fronted classic rock, country, and blues band based out of Lewiston, Maine.

Blending powerful vocals with a wide range of crowd-favorite hits from the 70’s through the 90’s, they bring energy, attitude, and a full-band sound to every performance.

Whether you're booking a bar, event, or private party, MISBEHAVIN delivers a fun, high-quality live music experience.
