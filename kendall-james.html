Learn more about Kendall James

Kendall James is a lifelong musician and performing songwriter based in Houlton, Maine.

His work is rooted in rock and roll, Americana, and song-driven music, with influences ranging from classic artists of the 60s through today to songwriters and bands including The Eagles, Tom Petty, Lyle Lovett, and Jason Isbell.

Availability

Kendall is available for some fill-in gigs or sideman tours, but also does studio work. He can provide solo tracks, single guitar tracks, or fully mixed tracks from his studio in Houlton.
