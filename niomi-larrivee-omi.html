Learn more about Niomi Larrivee / Omi

Niomi Larrivee, also known as Omi, is a vocalist based in Augusta, Maine who is looking to start a new band.

She has been singing professionally for four years and brings a strong range with a low, sultry, gritty voice.

Niomi can perform across many genres including rock, alternative, pop, blues, country, R&B, and rap. She is especially interested in starting an alternative new-country rock band, but is open to all opportunities.
