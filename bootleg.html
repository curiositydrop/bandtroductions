Learn more about Bootleg

Bootleg is starting fresh as a three-piece project after several years playing together in a previous band. The group is building something new with a laid-back approach, a love of music, and a focus on classic rock, 80’s rock, and country.

The group typically chooses a few songs to practice individually between rehearsals, then brings everything together when they meet. Their goal is to get together about once a month for practice or gigs during the summer months, occasionally more, and a couple times a month or more during the winter.

Bootleg is looking to build something fun and pleasing to the ears by adding a lead guitarist and drummer. Backing vocals or the ability to handle some lead vocals would be a huge plus.
