Learn more about Mike Spencer

Mike Spencer is a seasoned drummer and percussionist based in the Clifton/Bangor area of Maine.

With professional gear, years of experience, and a reliable attitude, Mike brings a strong background in rock and blues along with some country experience.

Influenced by legendary artists like Steely Dan, Deep Purple, Pink Floyd, and KISS, Mike combines technical skill with a deep appreciation for classic musicianship.
