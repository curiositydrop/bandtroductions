Learn more about Black Rabbit Holes

BRH is the highly self-indulgent recording project of Eric Hartmann and a rotating cast of adventurous collaborators.

The project blends elements of rock, hard rock, metal, prog, emo, and alternative into a unique sound that refuses to stay in one lane.

As Eric puts it: “What if Fall Out Boy’s favorite band was Tool?” Probably not a question anyone asked — but Black Rabbit Holes answers it anyway.

If you dig the sound and want to collaborate, reach out and throw down.
