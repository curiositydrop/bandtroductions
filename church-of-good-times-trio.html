Learn more about Church of Good Times Trio

The Church of Good Times is a contemporary instrumental jazz/jam trio exploring the FUN-damentals of sound and the FUN-KEY-ness of collective creative expression.

Focusing on improvised and accessible present-moment music composition, they provide live music for clubs, festivals, house parties, and community gatherings of all kinds.

Get up and dance or lay back and groove — all are welcome. The trio also recently released their latest album, Elemental Mysteries.

Band Members

  • Rev. Todd Ketcham – Bass
  • Rev. Todd Glacy – Drums
