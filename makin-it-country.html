Learn more about Makin' It Country

Makin' It Country is a five-piece country band from Central Maine performing a variety of country music favorites.

The group features multiple vocalists performing both lead vocals and harmonies, bringing an energetic and entertaining live experience to venues and special events.

Band Members

  • Dennis Royer – Steel Guitar
  • Donnie Duplissis – Lead & Rhythm Guitar
  • Marcel Caron – Bass Guitar
  • Diane Hanscom – Drums
  • Butch Tardif – Sound
