Learn more about Project Firebird

Project Firebird is a six-piece cover band rooted in classic rock with an expanding catalog spanning multiple decades and genres by popular request.

Based in Portland, Maine, the band performs across Maine and beyond for public and private events.

Project Firebird also works with specialty and costume themes, including energetic 80’s nights designed to get the crowd moving and the party started.

Band Members

  • Shanna Walker – Vocals / Percussion
  • Steve Saucier – Bass / Backup Vocals
  • Ryan Brown – Vocals
  • JT Leonard – Guitar / Backup Vocals
  • Jay Ferrell – Electric / Acoustic Guitar / Backup Vocals
  • Logan Cutlip – Drums
