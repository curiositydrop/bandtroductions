Learn more about Scotty Saints and the TrueBelievers

Scotty Saints and the True Believers is a melodic skate punk band from Portland, Maine, formed in 2018, known for a high-energy 1990s sound. Signed to Ninety Four Records, they blend fast punk rock with heartfelt, personal lyrics. Key releases include the album Hang On in 2021 and a series of 2024 singles leading to a 2025 vinyl release, Zeroes and Ones.
