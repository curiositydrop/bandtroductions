Learn more about Thomas Charles Clukey

Thomas Charles Clukey is a singer-songwriter from Saco, Maine whose music is deeply rooted in the landscapes and stories of New England.

Growing up in St. Albans, Thomas was immersed in music from an early age — from listening to country radio with his dad to performing in school band and learning from teachers who helped nurture his passion.

With a sound that blends country, roots, Americana, and folk, Thomas crafts songs that feel like heart-to-heart conversations. His influences range from Chris Stapleton and Tyler Childers to John Denver.

His lyrics reflect the grit and grace of everyday life, often exploring how music can serve as therapy while honoring his Maine roots.

Highlights

  • Winner of the Wildwood Studio Songwriting Contest in Brooklyn, NY
  • Winner of The Studio Portland Singer/Songwriter Contest
  • Performed at Erik's Church, Elements, Smoked in Portland, and Songwriters in the Round at Blue
  • Collaborating with Plaid Dog Studio in Boston, MA on a debut album
  • Recent single: “Freedom's Calling”
