Learn more about Country Roads

Country Roads is a highly professional, family-friendly Southern Maine modern country band that has been performing throughout Maine and New Hampshire for over 11 years.

The band has a well-established Facebook page with nearly 5,000 followers and a large collection of performance videos available to view there.
