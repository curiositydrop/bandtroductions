Learn more about Sygnal to Noise

Sygnal to Noise is a melodic hard rock band founded in 2013 by frontman Coopa, a seasoned singer/songwriter in the New England music scene.

Blending influences from Queen, KISS, Stone Temple Pilots, David Bowie, Prince, Alice In Chains, and Foo Fighters, STN delivers a powerful mix of melody and hard-hitting riffs while keeping true rock ‘n’ roll alive.

Known for high-energy, no-tracks, no-autotune live performances, the band prides itself on authenticity and raw stage presence.

🏆 Highlights

  • Winner: Best Hard Rock/Metal Band (2015) – New England Music Awards
  • Multiple nominations: New England Music Awards (2014, 2016, 2019, 2021)
  • Portland Music Awards nominee
  • International nomination (UK – TBFMOnline)
  • Multiple Chaos Zyne honors including Best Vocalist & Fan Favorite

Band Members

  • Coopa – Lead Vocals / Guitar
  • Brandon Tardiff – Drums / Backup Vocals
  • Geoff Flemming – Guitar / Keyboard / Backup Vocals
  • Thomas Siracusa – Bass
  • Bobby Emmons – Lead Guitar
