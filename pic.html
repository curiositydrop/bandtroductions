Learn more about Paradise is Cancelled

Paradise Is Cancelled has been bringing their hard-hitting sound to stages across New England since 2018. Blending elements of hard rock and nu metal, they’ve been described as Hellyeah meets Slipknot — delivering a powerful mix of melody and aggression.

With a loyal fan base and a dynamic dual-vocalist front, the band creates an intense, high-energy experience every time they perform. Their mission is simple: take their sound as far as it can go — and play for the world.

Band Members

  • Brandon Bones – Vocals
  • Teejay Gunn – Drums
